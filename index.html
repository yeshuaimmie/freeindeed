About the Author

John Mugabi
Pastor, Mentor & Author

John Mugabi is a born-again Christian, pastor, mentor, husband to Suzan and father to Zoe, Zion and Zeal. Born and raised in Kampala, he is the co-founder of Boys Blueprint Mentorship, an organisation guiding young men in purity, character and faith.

Professional Background

He is a PRINCE II certified Cost Engineer and Project Manager with over 10 years of experience in real estate and construction. His professional expertise brings practical discipline to his ministry work.

In his free time, he enjoys football, family vacations and playful moments with his children, cherishing opportunities to strengthen his home and faith.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Our Mission

"To Help Individuals Claim Victory Over Their Mind, Free Their Souls and Live in Victory through biblical teaching, counseling, and community support."
