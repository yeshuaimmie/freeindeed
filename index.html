Truth That Sets You Free

You need to know these life-changing truths about freedom from addiction

It's very possible to defeat Porn and Lust and regain a life of victory

True freedom is not a myth. Through Christ's power and biblical principles, complete victory is achievable.

Don't let porn give you a fake identity - Jesus defined your identity

You are not defined by your struggles. In Christ, you are a new creation - righteous, holy, and beloved.

Your best days are not behind you; they are ahead of you

God has a future and a hope for you. The past doesn't determine your destiny in Christ.

When you feel stuck and relapsing, don't give up - Let Jesus be your anchor

His grace is sufficient, and His strength is made perfect in your weakness.

You are a victor, not a victim. Embrace the victory mindset

In Christ, you are more than a conqueror. Walk in the victory He has already won for you.

You can regain your relationships; your marriage can be reborn

You are not too far gone; you can be redeemed

No one is beyond the reach of God's grace and transforming power.

You are not a porn statistic; you are God's masterpiece

About the Author

John Mugabi is a born-again Christian, pastor, mentor, husband to Suzan and father to Zoe, Zion and Zeal. Born and raised in Kampala, he is the co-founder of Boys Blueprint Mentorship, an organisation guiding young men in purity, character and faith.

Professionally, he is a prince II certified Cost Engineer and Project Manager with over 10 years of experience in real estate and construction. In his free time, he enjoys football, family vacations and playful moments with his children, cherishing opportunities to strengthen his home and faith.
